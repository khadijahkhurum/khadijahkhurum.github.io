Cœur de Rose

Description:

Immerse yourself in the heart of elegance with the enchanting scent of pure rose and soft musk. This exquisite fragrance envelops you in a delicate and romantic embrace.

Additional Information:

Notes:

  • Top: Fresh Rose Petals
  • Heart: Pure Rose Essence
  • Base: Soft Musk

Fragrance Family: Floral

Occasion:

Ideal for romantic evenings or special celebrations, adding a touch of timeless beauty to your presence.

Intensity:

Light to Moderate

Experience:

Let Cœur de Rose transport you to a blooming rose garden, where the air is filled with the heady aroma of fresh roses mingling with the gentle warmth of soft musk. This fragrance is a celebration of elegance and romance.
