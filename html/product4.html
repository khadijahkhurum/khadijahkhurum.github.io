Étoile Filante

Description:

Bask in the enchanting glow of sparkling bergamot and delicate lily of the valley. This celestial fragrance captures the magic of a shooting star, enveloping you in a luminous and captivating embrace.

Additional Information:

Notes:

  • Top: Sparkling Bergamot
  • Heart: Lily of the Valley
  • Base: Soft Amber

Fragrance Family: Citrus, Floral

Occasion:

Perfect for any special occasion, adding a touch of celestial elegance to your presence.

Intensity:

Light to Moderate

Experience:

Let Étoile Filante transport you to a night sky illuminated by a brilliant shooting star. The invigorating blend of bergamot and lily of the valley creates a radiant fragrance that captures the awe-inspiring beauty of a fleeting celestial moment.
