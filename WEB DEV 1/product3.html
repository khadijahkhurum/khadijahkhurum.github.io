Vérité Nocturne

Description:

Unveil the nocturnal truth with the exquisite scent of white orchid and soft sandalwood. This enchanting fragrance captures the essence of midnight blooms, enveloping you in a serene and mysterious embrace.

Additional Information:

Notes:

  • Top: White Orchid
  • Heart: Midnight Blooms
  • Base: Soft Sandalwood

Fragrance Family: Floral, Woody

Occasion:

Ideal for evening events or intimate gatherings, invoking an aura of elegance and intrigue.

Intensity:

Moderate

Experience:

Allow Vérité Nocturne to guide you through the mystical realm of the night, where the intoxicating fragrance of white orchids intertwines with the deep, comforting notes of sandalwood. This fragrance is an invitation to explore the hidden truths of the nocturnal world.
