Éclat de Lune

Description:

Experience the enchanting allure of night-blooming jasmine and velvety plum. This captivating fragrance captures the shimmering essence of moonlight, enveloping you in a mysterious and luminous embrace.

Additional Information:

Notes:

  • Top: Night-blooming Jasmine
  • Heart: Velvety Plum
  • Base: Soft Sandalwood

Fragrance Family: Floral, Oriental

Occasion:

Perfect for evening wear, adding a touch of mystique to your nighttime adventures.

Intensity:

Moderate to Strong

Experience:

Let Éclat de Lune transport you to a moonlit garden, where the delicate fragrance of jasmine mingles with the richness of plum under the soft glow of the moon. This fragrance is a journey into the magical realms of the night.
