Lumière Dorée

Description:

Immerse yourself in the radiant essence of sun-kissed citrus and golden peach. This luminous fragrance captures the warmth and vibrancy of a sunlit orchard, enveloping you in a delightful fruity embrace.

Additional Information:

Notes:

  • Top: Sun-kissed Citrus
  • Heart: Golden Peach
  • Base: Warm Musk

Fragrance Family: Fruity

Occasion:

Perfect for daytime wear, adding a touch of sunshine to your everyday moments.

Intensity:

Light to Moderate

Experience:

Let Lumière Dorée transport you to a serene orchard bathed in golden light, where the air is filled with the tantalizing aroma of ripe fruits. This fragrance is a celebration of life's radiant moments.
