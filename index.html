Our Identity

Indulge in the epitome of luxury and refinement at our prestigious French luxury perfumery nestled in the heart of Nice. Inspired by Parisian perfumery, our scents are meticulously crafted by master perfumists, each bottle a testament to sophistication and allure. Exclusively designed for women who dare to embrace their individuality and exude confidence, our fragrances captivate the senses, leaving a lingering impression wherever they go. For women who dare, our perfumery invites you to embrace the daring spirit that defines our identity.

Meet the CEO

Marie-Claire Dubois

Marie-Claire Dubois, the visionary CEO of Maison de Juliette. As the daughter of renowned perfumer Jacques Dubois, she carries forward a legacy of craftsmanship and passion, rooted in a lineage of distinguished parfumists. Under her leadership, the maison blends tradition with innovation, crafting scents that capture the essence of French elegance. Marie-Claire's dedication ensures each fragrance tells a unique story, resonating with timeless sophistication.
