Our Origins

Maison de Juliette began as a small family-owned business, founded in the heart of Grasse, France, the perfume capital of the world. Our founders, Juliette and Pierre, were passionate about capturing the essence of their surroundings in exquisite fragrances.

In the charming streets of Nice, France, Maison de Juliette was born from a love story between scent and sensation. Over a century ago, Jacques Dubois, a gifted perfumer, created a fragrance for his beloved Juliette, capturing their romance in a bottle. This scent, a blend of Provence’s lavender fields and the Riviera’s sea breeze, became the foundation of a family legacy. Passed down through generations, each perfumer added their touch, perfecting the art of crafting emotions into fragrances. Today, led by Marie-Claire Dubois, the maison continues to enchant, weaving tradition and innovation into every exquisite creation. Welcome to Maison de Juliette, where every scent tells a story.
